Hello World

Author: Haol Date: May 7, 2020  21:34:45

Hi, I’m Haoliang. I’m an actor, a singer, a Buddhist monk and a freedom fighter. I write news, take vows, teach, and conduct dance, martial arts and songs in the streets, in apartments, clinics, markets and playgrounds. So if you find me on the streets, look out! I’m there to help you get your free will back, or to rob you blind!

Working as an artist is time-consuming. I’ve got other obligations and people that I care about at home. I need to be able to make money somehow. I have no money, I’m in debt. I’m basically in an impossible position where it’s either you feel like you’re working against your best interests or that you’re lucky, you’ve got this big one and then you might get another big one, because you really can’t stop, you’ve got to keep selling your work and continue to starve.

I’m also a urn container! I recommend you add color to mine with red, yellow or white ribbon or piping. (this will make it a treasured gift or keep it for yourself). I will continue to add color to mine as time goes on. So don’t be afraid to add a little bit of whatever you’d like to see on mine.

I have been to many places. I’ve learned how to live in any neighborhood, in any situation. I’ve watched, grown and built good relationships. I’ve seen my childhood burned down to rubble. I’ve been in jail, prison and on the streets. I’ve shared compassion, I’ve demonstrated skills and lived among people who are at the bottom of the socioeconomic ladder. I’ve built good relationships. I’ve witnessed the destruction of others. I’ve been knocked down. I’ve been thrown up. I’ve laughed and cried. I have seen the pain. I have been in the streets to support others.

I’m Haoliang. I was thinking about what should I write to introduce me in my very first blog. There is not much to tell, so I inputted the first sentence to the language model I’ve been using for my master thesis and let it generates the rest.
